Welcome to Orbinova Academy

Dear Esteemed Parents, Students, and Well-Wishers, I am honored to address you as the Director of Orbinova Academy, an institution dedicated to nurturing academic excellence and shaping the future stars of the medical and engineering fields. With a rich legacy as the Secretary of Urdu Education Society Akola since 1960, overseeing 7 high schools, a Junior College, a B Ed College, and a District Level Central Government Scheme of vocational training, our commitment to quality education has stood the test of time. I am delighted to announce the inception of Orbinova Academy, an extension of our educational endeavors, providing comprehensive coaching for JEE, NEET, MHT-CET, and specialized 11th and 12th capsule batches in PCB, PCM, and PCBM. Our academy is distinguished by a cadre of exceptional faculty, comprised of IITians, doctors, and seasoned educators with extensive experience in JEE and NEET coaching. At Orbinova Academy, we take pride in our top-notch infrastructure, offering state-of-the-art facilities such as digital interactive whiteboards, air-conditioned classrooms, both online and offline teaching options, and an 86-cubicle library with revolving chairs. Situated in the heart of the city, in the upscale Ratanlal plot, we provide easy accessibility, being within walking distance from the necklace road, less than half a kilometer from the bus stand and railway station. This strategic location ensures convenience for students, facilitating an environment conducive to learning. Orbinova Academy is not just an educational institution; it is a place where dreams take flight. We are committed to shaping the future leaders in the medical and engineering fields, empowering them with knowledge, skills, and a passion for excellence. Join us on this transformative journey at Orbinova Academy, where the seeds of success are sown, and future stars are born..

Privacy policy

Information That We Collect

We may collect personal information, including but not limited to names, contact details, academic history, and preferences, provided voluntarily through forms or other interactive features on our website. Additionally, we may automatically collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser details, and usage patterns.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to personalize user experience, improve our website, communicate with you, process applications, and provide relevant information about our educational services. Your data is treated with utmost confidentiality and is not shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and gather information about usage patterns. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings, but disabling cookies may affect certain website functionalities.

Changes To Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page, and it is your responsibility to review the policy periodically.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites for reference or convenience. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content on these external sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of linked websites before providing any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Orbinova Academy does not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe that a child has provided us with their personal information, please contact us, and we will promptly take appropriate action.

Security

We prioritize the security of your information and employ industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security, and users share information at their own risk.
